Task a.

I chose a video featuring Professor Mustafa Baydoğan to write this brief summary. I took his classes during my undergraduate studies at Boğaziçi University, and I really like both his personality and his teaching style.

In this video, the professor mainly talks about data-driven decision-making processes. He uses examples that are similar to case studies to show how these processes are applied in different sectors based on his own professional experiences. In addition to these examples, he explains the different stages of data analysis. He also compares the traditional methods used in decision-making with deep learning methods. He covers their similarities and differences, as well as the advantages and disadvantages of each. He even talks about how reinforcement learning works in a general sense, noting that it was a popular alternative at the time the video was recorded.

Finally, he mentions the new horizons reached because humans have expanded and advanced their work in data-driven decision-making. He gives two specific examples of these new areas: physics-informed machine learning and machine learning for optimization.
